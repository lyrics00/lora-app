📝 Preparing Your Dataset

  • 🖼️ Collect at least 30–100 images of your subject.
  • 🧹 Clean the data — remove blurry, irrelevant, or duplicate images.
  • 💬 Caption your images using tools like BLIP or manual tagging.
  • 🗂️ Organize into folders: images/ and captions/.
  • 📁 Zip the folders and upload to Colab or your training environment.
