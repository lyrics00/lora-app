📝 Preparing Your Dataset

  • 🖼️ Collect: Aim for at least 30–100 high-quality images of your subject.
  • 🧹 Clean: Remove blurry, irrelevant, or duplicate images.
  • 💬 Caption: Use tools like BLIP or add descriptions manually.
  • 🗂️ Organize: Place images and captions in separate folders — images/ and captions/.
  • 📁 Package: Zip your folders and upload them to Google Colab or your chosen training setup.
