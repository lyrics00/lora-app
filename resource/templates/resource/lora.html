🛠️ Tips for Better Results

  • 📏 Use 512x512 resolution images unless your model supports larger sizes.
  • 🧹 Clean datasets = cleaner results. Remove blurry, unrelated, or duplicate images.
  • 🧪 Experiment with learning rate and training steps – too high can overfit, too low won’t converge.
  • 💾 Save intermediate checkpoints to compare results.
  • 🔁 Test your LoRA in different prompts to see generalization.
